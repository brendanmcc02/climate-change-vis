Analytics - Ireland






Interestingly, it's very difficult to spot a correlation between rainfall and temperature in Ireland. You will see that in other countries, there is an obvious correlation between rainfall and temperature. I think this is the case because since Ireland is a very small island (in comparison to other countries), the temperature of the island is relatively consistent. However, the size of a country does not impact the amount of rainfall. Hence, it is difficult to spot a correlation between rainfall and temperature. Moreover, you can see the temperature and tourism drop in a very similar way from 2007 - 2010. Obviously, this was during the Irish recession in 2008, but I found it interesting that the temperature would also drop in an economic crisis. My guess is that there was less fossil-fuel production in Ireland after the recession, hence the drop in temperature.
