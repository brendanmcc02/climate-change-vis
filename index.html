The Impact Of Climate Change On Tourism

Home

Hello and Welcome. This is a website where I hope both you and I can learn something new about the climate we live in today. Through visualisations and analysis, I hope to find a correlation between Tourism Statistics, National Temperatures and National Rainfall. With the navigation above, you'll see interactive graphs for various countries and continents, including Ireland, Europe, Asia and America. You can also see written conclusions and analysis for each graph with the click of a button. If you wish to contact me by email, you may do so on the about page.
